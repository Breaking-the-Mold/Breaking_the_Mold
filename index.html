About Us

Women can code. Its not a question but a reality.

Techology is a male dominated field, no denying that. We're seeking to Break the Mold by helping women, who want to leap into the language or development stack of their choice, regardless of their age or experience level.

No matter your industry or interests, right now is a great time to become a woman in technology, the eyes of the world are on us and its time to stop putting it off --- there is no time like the present to get good!
